Random Meal Generator

Welcome to the Random Meal Generator! Are you tired of the same old meals and need some inspiration in the kitchen? Look no further. With just a click of a button, our generator will provide you with exciting and diverse meal ideas to spice up your culinary adventures.

Individual Project

Website: Github Repo

Description: The Random Meal Generator is your go-to tool for those moments when you can't decide what to cook. Whether you're looking for a quick weekday dinner or planning a special meal, our generator will surprise you with a wide range of recipes from various cuisines.

Project Requirements

  • Utilize an API-integrated database to generate food names and accompanying images.
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation.
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop devices.

    • Project Overview

    The Random Meal Generator website is a React-based project that leverages an API-integrated database to offer users an interactive platform for generating random meal ideas. With a seamless user experience, the website provides a vast collection of culinary options, complete with accompanying images and detailed recipes sourced from the integrated database.
