Retroland

Welcome to Retroland, your ultimate destination for all things vintage and retro. Immerse yourself in a world of nostalgia as we bring you a carefully curated collection of products inspired by the iconic styles and trends of the past. From retro-inspired gadgets to nostalgic memorabilia, explore a treasure trove of unique items that celebrate the timeless charm and distinctive aesthetics of bygone eras.

Website: Github Repo

Description: Retroland is your premier online store for vintage and retro-themed products that capture the essence of past eras. Discover a wide range of items that embody the spirit of nostalgia and celebrate the enduring appeal of vintage culture.

Project Requirements

  • Utilize PERN stack and Redux to develop the web application.
  • Integrate a payment gateway to faciliate secure online transactions.
  • Implement the shop's API routes, store products, and cart.

    • Project Overview

    Retroland is an exciting web development project that utilizes the power of the PERN stack (PostgreSQL, Express, React, Node.js) and Redux to create a robust and immersive ecommerce platform. With a focus on delivering a seamless user experience, the project aims to integrate a secure payment gateway to facilitate hassle-free online transactions. The development process involves implementing the shop's API routes, storing and managing products, and building a user-friendly shopping cart feature. Retroland is set to become the go-to destination for vintage and retro enthusiasts, providing them with a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience while indulging in their love for all things nostalgic.
